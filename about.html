About Michael

My name is Michael Rolls, I am an osteopath, pain relief and rehabilitation specialist.

I trained for 4 years to be an osteopath, learning about the body, how it works and how amazing it is. Part of that training included screening for conditions that need another healthcare practitioner or further testing. You don’t need a referral for a consultation with me.

Recently I spent 9 months learning more about exercise rehabilitation and the importance of tailoring plans to an individual’s circumstances. I am excited by getting people moving again, so they can enjoy the things that they may have stopped doing.

  • Osteopaths in the UK are regulated by the General Osteopath Council.
  • I graduated with distinction from the University College of Osteopathy.
  • I am a member of the Institute of Osteopathy.

Why choose Michael?

I offer personalised care: Not just the same thing I told the last person. Everyone wants and needs different things. Each person responds differently to any intervention and so any interaction will bear that in mind.

I want to give you the tools to help yourself: I can help you understand what is happening and what options you have. The aim, however, is for you to take control and learn strategies to improve your health so that you don’t need me any more.

Pain is an experience we all have. Why and how it happens is due to many factors. Our beliefs and attitudes affect how we feel within our bodies as well as the everyday realities of our lives. I won't just ask about what hurts but how it is affecting you, what you are missing and what you want to get out of our time together.

I run courses on using reciprocal inhibition and reciprocal inhibition as an adjunct to stretching. Get in touch for more details.
